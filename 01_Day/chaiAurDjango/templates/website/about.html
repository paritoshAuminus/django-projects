About Chai Aur Django

Welcome to Chai Aur Django — a web application created to help learners explore Django concepts while enjoying their cup of chai.

This project demonstrates Django fundamentals, templates, static files, and routing. Whether you're a beginner or leveling up your web development skills, Chai Aur Django is here to guide you.

Sit back, relax, grab a chai — and start building with Django!
